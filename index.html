Dhurandar

Dhurandhar, directed by Aditya Dhar (Uri: The Surgical Strike), is an intense, gritty espionage thriller film inspired by true events related to the India-Pakistan conflict and R&AW covert operations. Released in December 2025, the film is the first of a two-part saga.

Avatar: Fire And Ash

Avatar 3's New Villain Teased by James Cameron | The DirectAvatar 3, titled Avatar: Fire and Ash, follows Jake & Neytiri's family grieving Neteyam's death, facing a new, fire-loving Na'vi tribe (the Ash People/Mangkwan), led by Varang, who allies with Quaritch, escalating conflicts with major battles involving RDA, exploring darker themes of grief, hatred, & "good/bad" humans & Na'vi, focusing on Spider's role, Kiri's spiritual struggles, & a potential alliance between Quaritch & the Ash People against Jake's clan

Jolly LLB 3

Jolly LLB 3 is a legal drama about two rival lawyers, Jagdish "Jolly" Tyagi (Akshay Kumar) and Jagdishwar "Jolly" Mishra (Arshad Warsi), who clash in court over a case involving land acquisition and farmer suicides. The film follows the two Jollys as they eventually join forces to fight against a corrupt businessman who is trying to seize a village's land for development. The plot is centered on social issues like land acquisition and farmer distress, and the film is available to stream on Netflix as of November 14, 2025

Popular Series

Stranger Things(Season 5)

Stranger Things is a nostalgic sci-fi horror series set in the 1980s town of Hawkins, Indiana, about kids and teens uncovering supernatural mysteries, government conspiracies, and an alternate dimension (the Upside Down) after a young boy vanishes, leading to encounters with monsters and a psychokinetic girl named Eleven

IT: Welcome To Derry

It: Welcome to Derry is an HBO prequel series to Stephen King's It, set in 1962, exploring the origins of the evil, shape-shifting entity (Pennywise) in the town of Derry, Maine, focusing on a young boy's disappearance, a family new to town (including the psychic Hallorann's family), and the burning of a local nightclub, delving into Derry's dark history and the monster's terrifying presence through supernatural horror, body horror, and period drama

Asur

Asur is a gripping Indian web series blending forensic crime-solving with Hindu mythology, following CBI forensic experts {Dhananjay Rajpoot} (Arshad Warsi) and {Nikhil Nair} (Barun Sobti) as they hunt a cunning serial killer whose motives and murders are rooted in ancient Indian scriptures, particularly the story of the demon Asur. The show explores themes of good vs. evil, destiny, and the inner darkness (Asur) within humanity, with the killer provoking Nikhil to become his "Sarathi" (charioteer) in a larger mythological game
